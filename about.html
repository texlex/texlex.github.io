Experience

My dad, my uncle and my grandfathers were all bakers. Against my grandmother's advice, I became a lawyer. I made the right decision. Sorting out complicated issues among opponents proved far more interesting that sorting hot rolls. I do my job because I like it. I get my clients good results, and they like that. Litigation, intellectual property (trademarks and copyrights) and other legal services are what I provide. Call or write. If I cannot help you, I probably know someone who can.
